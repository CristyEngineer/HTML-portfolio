Ingredients

  • 300 g pasta
  • 400 g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic.
  2. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the carrot, diced tomatoes, and tomato paste.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Let the sauce simmer for 20–30 minutes.
  6. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  7. Serve the pasta topped with the Bolognese sauce.
