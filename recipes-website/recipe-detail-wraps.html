Ingredients

  • 4 large wraps
  • 300 g cooked chicken (shredded or sliced)
  • 150 g crème fraîche
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 handful of lettuce or mixed greens
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Spread a generous spoonful of crème fraîche over each wrap.
  2. Add the cooked chicken evenly across the wraps.
  3. Top with sliced bell pepper and fresh greens.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Fold the sides in and roll the wrap tightly.
  6. Slice in half and serve immediately.

Nutrient Amount
