Where Mom Learned to Cook

Read the story

My mom learned to cook during survival camps when she was a kid. That’s why many of her recipes can be made in just one pan — simple, practical, and full of flavor.

Why Mom Loved Sunday Cooking

Read the story

Every Sunday, the house smelled like simmering soup and fresh herbs. Mom always said that cooking slowly was her way of slowing down the week.

Fun Fact: The Secret Ingredient

Reveal the secret

Mom believed that adding a spoonful of crème fraîche to almost anything made it “a little softer, a little kinder.”

Kitchen Tip: Keep It Simple

Show tip

Mom always said: “If the ingredients are good, the recipe doesn’t need to be complicated.” A reminder we can all use.
