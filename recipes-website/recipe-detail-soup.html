Ingredients

  • 300 g chicken (shredded or diced)
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 liter chicken broth
  • 1 handful of mixed vegetables
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat a little oil in a pot and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the carrots and potatoes and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add the chicken and reduce the heat to a simmer.
  5. Let the soup cook for 20–25 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
